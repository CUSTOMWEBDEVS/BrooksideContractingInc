Overview

Professional landscape construction practice near St. Louis, focused on water features and estate stonework, serving residential and commercial clients with principal-led delivery.

Brookside has specialized for decades in the design and construction of waterfalls, streams, ponds, fountains, and natural swimming pools. We also execute boulder gardens, stone walls and pathways, and outdoor entertainment areas.

Our approach blends artistic vision, design detail, and sound construction to create scenes that appear naturally formed. We collaborate with top professionals and have delivered high-profile work across Missouri, Illinois, and Kansas.

A seasoned field team brings 100+ years of combined experience, coordinating with architects and designers to meet specifications precisely. We believe in Michael Littlewood’s charge to “plan and design in harmony with nature”—and extend it to how we build.
